About Us

Queens of Change Foundation is a women-led organisation working to uplift underprivileged women and girls across India. We believe that every woman — no matter where she is born — deserves access to basic health resources, education, and the chance to build a better life.

Our work spans free sanitary pad distribution, menstrual health awareness, and skill-building programmes that help women become independent and confident. We don't just provide resources — we walk alongside the communities we serve.

Registered under The Indian Societies Act 1860, we are committed to transparent, grassroots-driven change — one woman, one village at a time.
